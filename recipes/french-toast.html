French Toast

Ingredients:

  • 6 pieces square milk or egg bread
  • 4 tablespoons heaping peanut butter
  • 2 eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Butter
  • Condensed Milk, honey or maple syrup

Description:

A popular Hoong Kong cafe dessert. Layered french toast stuffed with peanut butter, coated in egg and shallow fried.

Recipe credits:

  • Hong Kong style french Toast

Steps:

  1. With 3 pieces of bread, spread peanut butter on 2 of the 3 pieces. Sandwich them together. Repeat for the other sandwich.
  2. Slice off the crusts of your sandwiches to create a square.
  3. Whisk 2 eggs.
  4. Fill a shallow layer of cooking oil in a deep pan. Bring it to low medium heat.
  5. Lightky coat one sanwich in egg.
  6. Lower that sandwich into the hot oil. Fry on all sides until golden brown. Repeat the above step for second sandwich.
  7. Enjoy hot with butter and sweetener of your choice!
