Egg Tart

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs, beaten, reserve 2 tablespoons for your pastry dough
  • ⅛ teaspoon pinch salt
  • ¾ cup hot water
  • Vanilla extract, a dash, optional
  • 6 tablespoons sugar

Description:

A popular Hoong Kong cafe dessert. Layered French toast stuffed with peanut butter, coated in egg and shallow fried.

Recipe credits:

  • Hong Kong Style Egg Tarts

Steps:

  1. For the pastry, mix sift flour, sugar, salt, softened butter in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk the egee yalks and add 2 tablespoons of beaten yolk to the flour mixture. Cover with plastic wrap and then refigrtate for 30 minutes.
  3. Make custard filling, melt sugar and salt with hot water.
  4. Add the rest of the beaten egg yolk. Stir and combine everything well.
  5. Chill them in the refrigerator.
  6. Preheat the oven to 200 degree.
  7. Take the dough out and divide into 16 equal portions. Try to make the wrapper uniform in thickness and avoid a thick bottom.
  8. Pour the custard fillimg into the shells until it about 80%. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes until the surface becomes golden brown.
  9. Cool down for several minutes and then take the egg tarts out of the pan.
  10. Enjoy!!
