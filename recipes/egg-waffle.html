Egg Waffle

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all purpose flour (4 oz)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon corn starch
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons evaporated milk
  • 5 oz water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Description:

A popular Hoong Kong cafe dessert. Layered french toast stuffed with peanut butter, coated in egg and shallow fried.

Recipe credits:

  • How to Make Hong Kong Egg Cakes

Steps:

  1. Mix together the flour, baking powder, corn starch in a large bowl.
  2. Add the eggs, sugar, vegetable oil, and vanilla. Mix by hand.
  3. Pour in the evaporated milk and mix thoroughly.
  4. Let it stand for an hour helped dissolve the lumps.
  5. Set a wire rack on a baking sheet.
  6. Pre-heat each half of the Nordic Ware Egg Waffle Pan for 5 minutes.
  7. Lightly brush with vegetable oil. Lower heat to medium.
  8. Pour Â¾ cup of the batter into the middle of the egg waffle pan.
  9. Immediately place the other side of the pan on top, flip the pan over and cook for 2 to 2.5 minutes.
  10. Flip again and cook for a further 2 to 2.5 minutes.
  11. Open the pan and invert the eggettes onto the wire rack and let cool for a minute or so.
  12. Repeat with the remaining batter. Enjoy!!
